Premier League Calculator

Welcome to the match calculator. It can take any/all of the following factors into consideration when predicting who will win a proposed match: Current Standings (the current rank of both teams in the table), Fixtures' History (which team has won more of the last ten matches), and Home/Away Records (accounting for whether a team has been better or worse at home or away over the course of the current season).
